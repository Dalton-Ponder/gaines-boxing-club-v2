The Schedule page for Gaines Boxing Club. Showcase gym hours and events.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
The design MUST be strictly DARK MODE ONLY. The aesthetic is 'underground style' with concrete-like textures, gritty but premium and professional. The primary accent color is Kiln Orange (#C04E01). Use highly polished UI elements, use deep dark grays/blacks (#111, #1A1A1A) with striking Kiln Orange (#C04E01) typography, buttons, borders, or glowing accents to evoke the feel of a gritty yet premium underground boxing gym. Font: LEXEND. Roundness: ROUND_EIGHT.

**Page Structure:**

1. Header with navigation (Home, Legacy, Coaches, Philosophy, Schedule)
2. Hero section: "Schedule & Events"
3. Gym Hours Table/Section: Emphasize open hours (Mondays 5pm - 8pm, Thursdays 6pm - 8pm). Use a dark card with glowing orange accents to highlight the times.
4. Featured Event Section: large impactful card for a featured upcoming event (e.g. Golden Gloves Qualifier).
5. Past and Future Events: Grid beneath the featured event showing past highlights and upcoming local fights.
6. Footer with location, contact, and "Discover the legacy, embrace the discipline, and revel in the fun".
